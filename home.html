Our organization is the only student organization at UC Berkeley that represents all intersectional minority groups and unites us under one mission -- to professionally develop and create a safe space for Black, Latine, LGBTQ+, and all historically marginalized students. We accept all applicants and provide an open-resource weekly fellowship program which trains all students that come to us on how to recruit for careers in finance, consulting, technology, and more.
